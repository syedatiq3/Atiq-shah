Force system

A finite numbers of forces acting on a body is called a force system

Components of a forces

If two or more forces F1,F2,F3,....etc act on abody and if a single force R can be found whose effect on the bodyis the same as that forces F1,F2,F3,...etc.

A body under the action of two forces is in equilibrium if and only if those are equal in magnitude opposite in direction and are collinear.

Concurrent forces

The forces whose lines of action intersect at once point are called concurrent forces. Such forces always have a resultant through the point of concurrency.
